Refund & Cancellation Policy

Understand our refund and delivery terms

Last Updated: December 2024

Important: Since we sell digital products, please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. General Refund Policy

Due to the nature of digital products, all sales are final once the product access details have been delivered. We cannot offer refunds for:

  • Products that have been successfully delivered
  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Products purchased with incorrect email/contact details provided by customer

2. Eligible Refund Cases

We will consider refunds in the following situations:

  • Non-delivery: If we fail to deliver the product within 48 hours of payment verification
  • Defective Product: If the product/service doesn't work as described and we cannot provide a replacement
  • Duplicate Payment: If you were charged multiple times for the same order
  • Wrong Product: If you received a completely different product than ordered

3. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please:

  1. Contact us within 7 days of purchase
  2. Provide your order number and payment proof
  3. Explain the reason for refund request
  4. Allow us 3-5 business days to review your request

4. Refund Timeline

If your refund is approved:

  • We will initiate the refund within 24 hours
  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method
  • It may take 5-10 business days for the amount to reflect in your account

5. Cancellation Policy

Before Delivery: You can cancel your order before we deliver the product access details. Contact us immediately after placing the order.

After Delivery: Once product access details are shared, cancellation is not possible.

6. Delivery Policy

All our products are digital and delivered via:

  • Email to your registered email address
  • WhatsApp to your registered phone number
  • Telegram (if applicable)

Delivery Timeline: 2-24 hours after payment verification

Note: Ensure your email and phone number are correct when placing an order.

7. Disputes & Resolution

If you face any issues:

  1. Contact our support team first
  2. We will try to resolve the issue within 48 hours
  3. If not resolved, you may escalate through appropriate legal channels
